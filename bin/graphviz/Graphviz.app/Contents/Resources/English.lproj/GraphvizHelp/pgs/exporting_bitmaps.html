Exporting bitmaps

You can export the graph to different bitmap formats. The formats available depend on the QuickTime Export Components installed on your computer.

  • To export the graph as a single bitmap, choose the format from Export > File Format.

You should have at least Windows BMP, JPEG 2000, JPEG, MacPaint, QuickDraw PICT, PNG, Photoshop, QuickTime Image, SGI and TIFF available.
