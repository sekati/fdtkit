Changing choice settings

You can change choice settings using popup menus. Choice settings are found in the Graph, Node or Edge list. They are marked with an up-down arrow.

  • Click on the up-down arrow and choose from the menu that pops up. To get more information about a menu item, hover over it to see its tool tip.

You can also double-click the choice text and edit it directly. Graphviz automatically completes the choice text as you type.
